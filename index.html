The Product

Warmth That Doesn't
Compromise Safety.

Traditional forced-air warming systems pump heated air through perforated blankets — and straight into your sterile surgical field. ThermaBlanket uses resistive warming technology that delivers consistent, controlled heat directly to the patient, with no airflow, no turbulence, and no bacterial dispersal risk.

🌡️

Consistent, Controlled Warmth

Uniform heat distribution maintains normothermia throughout the procedure — from induction through to recovery — without the temperature spikes of forced-air systems.

🦠

No Air, No Risk

Our blankets produce zero airflow in the OR. No recirculation of floor-level bacteria. No turbulence in the sterile field. Just clean, safe warmth for every patient.

⚡

Ready with Your Existing Setup

No capital equipment purchase required. ThermaBlanket integrates with standard OR power infrastructure — a seamless switch that won't disrupt your theatre workflow.

💰

Significantly Lower Cost Per Case

ThermaBlanket delivers the same clinical outcomes as imported alternatives at a fraction of the consumable cost — without the import markups, currency volatility, or supply delays.
